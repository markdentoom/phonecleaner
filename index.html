Devì

Clean devices that nurture, clean nature for our future

Eco-friendly media device cleaners for a greener planet and a brighter future

Devì introduces the first ever 100% sustainable 5-in-1 media device cleaner made from a combination of biodegradable and recyclable materials

Company profile

Devì was founded in August 2022 by seven students from the Far Eastern University with the goal of creating eco-friendly cleaning products for media devices

Vision

Our goal is to enrich the technology industry and contribute in making the world greener by creating products that are as good for the environment as they are for your devices

Mission

We strive to provide sustainable state-of-the-art cleaning tools for media devices to protect them from building up dirt that can harm the function of the device

Products



5-in-1 Media Device Cleaner

The portable Devì 5-in-1 Media Device Cleaner™ is the flagship product of Devì. Its consists of five detachable parts and comes with a multi-functional microfiber pen case It is designed for every day carry and sports a durable bamboo exterior with a trendy design



Microfiber Storage Pouch

The Microfiber Storage Pouch™ is made from an absorbent microfiber cloth which makes it perfect for both wiping dust, smudges, and fingerprints off senstive parts of your media devices such as screens and camera lenses, as well as neatly storing your Devì 5-in-1 Media Device Cleaner™



Disinfectant Spray

The Devì Disinfectant Spray™ kills 99.9% of virusses and bacteria formed on the surfaces of media devices. It can also ease the removal of stubborn dirt and stains that dried onto your gadgets



Bristled Brush

The Devì Bristled Brush™ is an erect and stiff spoolie-inspired brush ideal for clearing small openings and spaces, like charger or audio ports, of large particles and dirt



Microbrush

The Devì Microbrush™ is a flexible brush with soft tiger grass bristles used for cleaning small spaces and tight spots like the ones between keys of your keyboard



Macro Brush

The retractable Devì Macro Brush™ is a tool for cleaning large spaces such the surfaces of screens and keyboards
