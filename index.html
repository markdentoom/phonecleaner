Company profile

Innovation has always been perceived as a way of improving the quality of life of every individual, yet in our quest for a more advanced civilization, we have also created problems that are heavily affecting our way of life. Plastic emission and plastic pollution itself has extended their impact on our society and continuously harm the environment we live in; furthermore, a pandemic emerged in our modern-day society is infecting those who are unknowingly exposed to the contagious virus, forcing everyone to stay at their own homes and be more engaged in the virtual world. In these trying times, we came up with a business plan that offers a solution to this problem, introducing “Deví” it is a five-in-one pen-like cleaning tool that provides people various ways of cleaning their devices. It offers inclusiveness, sustainability, durability, convenience, and protection. The main function of the product revolves mainly in cleaning the dirt from all of its possible entrances and, at the same time, protecting and prolonging the life expectancies of the device. Deví is a business entity that cultivates the principles of inclusiveness, innovation, sustainability, and cleanliness

5-in-1 media device cleaner

Deví offers 5 in 1 portable media device cleaning tool with a base made and cover made out of bamboo and consists of five detachable functional features; disinfectant spray, bristles and brushes crafted from tiger grass and packed in a microfiber cloth (pen case). It is an on-the-go product that provides convenience, safety and does look eye-catchy and trendy for media device users.



Disinfectant spray

gets rid of viruses, germs, and bacteria that are formed in the surfaces of the media devices. It can also ease the removal of dirt and stain that has dried up and stuck on the devices.



Microfiber pen case

has a dual purpose feature wherein it serves as a mini-organizer case/ purse and packaging to be used by consumers to keep them neat, prevent misplacement and essential tool for wiping media devices and at the same time, it is an absorbent and soft tool to wipe up the dirt and remove dust, smudge and fingerprints that can be used for the sensitive parts of a media device such as screens and surfaces.



Bristled brush

The Bristled brush is a spoolie-inspired brush being erect and stiff which is exemplary for penetrating small openings/ spaces to clean the junks and other large particles and dirt in the hardware ports of the media device.



Micro-brush

The Microbrush is a flexible tool having its soft bristles made out of tiger grass that could clean inaccessible small spaces and tight spots in a media device like the small spaces between keys of your keyboards.



Macro-brush

The retractable Macrobrush is a tool for cleaning wider and lighter spaces such as screen and keyboards and is suitable for any media device surface. The product offered is versatile covering not just disinfecting the media devices but also, consumers will benefit from its portability, tidiness, spotlessness and aesthetically pleasing appearance.
